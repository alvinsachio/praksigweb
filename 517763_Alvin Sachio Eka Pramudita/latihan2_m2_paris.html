Paris


Paris is the capital city of France. It is one of the most populous cities in Europe, with a metropolitan area housing over 12 million inhabitants.


Located on the River Seine, Paris has been a center of art, fashion, and culture for centuries. Its history dates back to ancient times when it was settled by the Parisii, a Gallic tribe, before becoming a major city under Roman rule.
