Tokyo is the capital city of Japan. It is the most populous metropolitan area in the world, with over 37 million people living in the Greater Tokyo Area.


Originally a small fishing village known as Edo, Tokyo became a prominent city in the early 17th century. It was later renamed and became the capital of Japan in 1868, growing into a global financial and cultural hub.
